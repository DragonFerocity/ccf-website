Men's Christian Campus House - Housing Information

Campus Approved Housing

Our men's residential facility was completed in the fall of 2000 and provides housing for 31 male students with five adjoining apartment units housing an additional 10 men behind the main house. We are approved by S&T as student housing for underclassmen.

Low cost, High fellowship

The CCH has 8 spacious bedrooms holding 3-4 residents each. In addition to these residential areas where residents sleep, study, and hang out, we also have a kitchen, lounge, recreational, and other study areas open to everyone. All this and much more at a fraction of the cost of living in the residential halls on campus.

Convenient Location

We are located just 1 block east of campus and within walking distance to downtown Rolla, a couple of city parks, and several restaurants.

No Application Fee!
