Staff

Allen Schofield

Allen Schofield has served as the campus minister since 1997. Allen is an avid St. Louis Cardinals fan, enjoys Mt. Dew, and eating ice cream with his daughter Lily. His wife, Chrissy, is a very gifted artist and pianist. But more than that, the Schofields are passionate about sharing the love of God with others.

Email Allen at allen@rollaccf.org

Andy Conklin

Andy Conklin joined us in July 2011 to partner with Allen and Shandi in mentoring the students at Missouri S&T and to help prepare them for a future of loving God and serving His people. He married his wife, Amy, in 2005 and they have three kids, Abby, Paige, & Crawford. In addition to loving God and his family, you will usually find Andy around campus refining and enjoying his two other favorite things: Espresso and Frisbee Golf.

Email Andy at andy@rollaccf.org

Shandi Harris

Shandi has been with us since 2010, serving as CCF's first women's campus minister. She is passionate about helping people view themselves as God does and appreciate Him more. When Shandi is not working with CCF she is usually helping with her church's high school students, reading, cooking, analyzing a movie, or harassing her husband, Ben. To her, life is an adventure and you are never guaranteed tomorrow, so she strives to live EVERY day as though it is her last, and to never let a moment pass her by with regret.

Email Shandi at shandi@rollaccf.org

Loretta Cornick

Loretta Cornick is the CCF Office Manager and feels blessed to be serving in this capacity with such incredible Christian people. She is a former Rolla business owner and has many years of office and financial experience on the campus of Missouri S&T. She enjoys working with college students because of their intelligence, individuality and energy. Some of her favorite things to do for fun are fishing, watching movies and spending time with her two granddaughters, Clara and Eloise. Loretta believes that every day is a gift from God, and what you do with that gift determines the path your life will take.

Email Loretta at secretary@rollaccf.org

Megan Goff

Megan joins the CCF staff as the women's intern for the fall of 2013. Megan loves helping girls see beauty in life and as people God has created in His image. She is a student at Saint Louis Christian College, but in her 'free time' she loves to be where the laughter is loudest, where the conversation is most thought-provoking, or where the food and fun are plenty. Megan sticks to the mottos, 'in all things love,' and 'enjoy the little things," ensuring that life is never boring!
