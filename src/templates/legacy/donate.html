Help Us Out

Construction is very much in progress. The old apartments are nearly gone - only a partial wall and a pile of rubble remain. Room 206 upstairs is now a framed laundry room with electrical and plumbing roughed in. The kitchen is being gutted in preparation of the changes. Summers are always financially tough and this summer is no exception; with all of this chaos going on; with additional bills for displacement housing, parking lot addition, and our typical summer activities and light usage - money is tight. Thanks for supporting the ministry of Christian Campus Fellowship through your financial gifts.

Monthly Gift

As a growing ministry, we have bills, salaries, and other regular expenses. There are some who choose to support the ministry with a designated monthly gift. These gifts help take away some of the financial stress on the business side of ministry. Knowing that each month a gift will be arrive via Pay Pal, direct withdrawal from your checking account, or a dependable check arriving via the USPS is comforting. Please consider donating monthly for a period of time.

One-Time Gift

One time gifts add excitement to the day. These gifts often come at just the right time - we've used timely gifts to pay off our main building debt just as we began construction on the expansion, to replace a copier/printer that just printed it's last page, and even to cover student activities such as opening week, thanksgiving banquet, and st pats. These one time gifts are often spawned from God speaking into the hearts of men and women to go above and beyond to take care of the ministry in Rolla.

Stock Transfer

Donating appreciated stock from your investment portfolio instead of cash is a unique and mutually beneficial way to give to the ministry. The deduction for a donation of property to charity is equal to the fair market value of the donated property. Where the donated property is "gain" property, the donor does not have to recognize the gain on the donated property. These rules allow for the "doubling up," so to speak, of tax benefits: A charitable deduction, plus avoiding tax on the appreciation in value of the donated property. Ask your financial adviser for more information on how to support Christian Campus Fellowship.
